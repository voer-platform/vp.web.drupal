Financial and Managerial Accounting

Instructor: Prof. George Plesko

Course Highlights

Helping students achieve the five course challenges is a thorough set of lecture notes, complemented by class overviews and necessary handouts. A complete quiz and midterm exam are also available, with solutions.

Course Description

Financial and Managerial Accounting is an intensive introduction to the preparation and interpretation of financial information for investors (external users) and managers (internal users) and to the use of financial instruments to support system and project creation. The course adopts a decision-maker perspective on accounting and finance with the goal of helping students develop a framework for understanding financial, managerial, and tax reports. Financial and Managerial Accounting is restricted to System Design and Management students.
